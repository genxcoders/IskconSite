Why lotus temple structure :

  • The lotus flower has been associated with Lord Krsna.
  • As lotus face, lotus feet, lotus eyes, soft lotus hands.
  • Lotus meaning spiritual growth
  • Out of the hundreds of ISKCON temples there is none, which is conceptualized on lotus

Plan of temple

  • Ancillary buildings will come up on 3 acres of land
  • 2 Acres of Vrindavan Theme Garden
  • Temple hall for 1000 persons
  • Cultural Hall for 1500 persons
  • Diorama, Interpretation centre
  • Multipurpose hall for 1000 persons, Audio video special effects learning exhibition/show
  • Hare Krsna spiritual mall, Govinda restaurant, Brahmachari Ashram, Guest House
